Our Methodology

The ScaleX Social Strategy Blueprint

Six phases, zero guesswork. From discovery to scale, every move is calculated.

01 — Discovery & Audit
02 — Strategy & Positioning
03 — Content Creation
04 — Publishing & Management
05 — Community Growth
06 — Optimize & Scale
PHASE 01

Discovery & Audit

We dissect your current presence, competitors, and industry benchmarks before touching a single piece of content.

  • Full social media audit across all channels
  • Competitor deep-dive: content, tone, frequency, engagement
  • Audience persona mapping: demographics + psychographics
  • Brand voice extraction and documentation
  • Opportunity gap identification per platform
PHASE 02

Strategy & Positioning

We build a custom 90-day content strategy defining what to say, where, when, and how.

  • Content pillars (3–5 brand themes) per platform
  • Channel-specific objectives & KPIs
  • Content calendar with publishing cadence
  • Brand tone-of-voice guidelines (bilingual AR/EN)
  • Campaign and promotion calendar alignment
PHASE 03

Content Creation

Our in-house team produces scroll-stopping content across every format — at scale.

  • Video production: Reels, TikToks, YouTube Shorts
  • Graphic design: static posts, carousels, infographics
  • Copywriting: captions, hashtag research, CTAs
  • Photography direction for product & lifestyle
  • Monthly approval cycle with 3-day revision SLA
PHASE 04

Publishing & Management

Precise scheduling at optimal times, with real-time monitoring across every channel.

  • Scheduled publishing via enterprise tool (peak-hour targeting)
  • Story & Reel daily scheduling
  • Community management: comments, DMs, mentions
  • Crisis monitoring & escalation protocol
  • Monthly content calendar delivered in advance
PHASE 05

Community Growth

We build an audience that actually cares — not just passive scroll-bys.

  • Targeted follow/engagement outreach strategy
  • Influencer collaboration & co-creation
  • UGC campaigns & hashtag community building
  • Giveaway and activation campaign management
  • Cross-promotion with complementary brands
PHASE 06

Optimize & Scale

Monthly deep-dive analytics reviews turn insights into compounding growth.

  • Monthly performance report: reach, engagement, follower growth
  • Content scoring: top posts dissected and replicated
  • A/B testing: hook variations, posting times, formats
  • Quarterly strategy refresh based on platform algorithm changes
  • Budget reallocation to highest-ROI channels
